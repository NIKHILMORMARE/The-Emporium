------ Products -------

Milk and milk products are an excellent source of vitamins and minerals, particularly calcium. Milk has significant amounts of vitamin A and B vitamins such as thiamine, riboflavin and nicotinic acid, but is a poor source of vitamin C and vitamin E. It also contains vitamin B12. Milk parlours have a very unique function in dairy farms. But milk parlour does more than just serve milk, it also plays a major role in making a place fashionable. Many milk parlour around have a vintage feel to it and that is the reason they are so popular. The milk parlour shops sell milk and milk products on there own name and exclusivity. They usually have some of the oldest and most exclusive recipes of milk products that can be found there.
